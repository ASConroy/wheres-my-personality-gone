The Last Time I Had a Conversation About Myself

By the author · Some time ago · 4 min read

I've been trying to remember the last time someone asked me what I was into, and I gave an answer that wasn't about sleep schedules, nappy brands, or the surprisingly nuanced world of toddler snacks. I think it was 2021. Maybe 2020. There was wine involved.

This site is my attempt to excavate that person. The one who had opinions about things. The one who stayed up too late doing things for no reason other than wanting to.
